# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

The primary audience is prospective enterprise customers and senior decision-makers in operations, technology, facilities, innovation, and business transformation. They arrive as self-guided visitors or in sales-led and event contexts, and need to understand how Ecosat applies to their operational reality. Existing customers, strategic partners, and other stakeholders are secondary audiences seeking a clear view of the connected Ecosat ecosystem.

## Product Purpose

This responsive public web experience presents Ecosat as a connected physical and digital operational system. It guides visitors from Integración—the physical world of infrastructure, connectivity, systems, and operational capabilities—into Sidón, where operational data becomes visibility, control, and intelligence. Duma is the intelligence layer within Sidón, never a separate or transversal Ecosat layer. Success is earned trust, relevant self-guided discovery, and a natural transition into a commercial conversation.

## Positioning

Ecosat connects the physical and digital worlds, turning connected operations into actionable decisions.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: **Request a conversation** / **Solicitar una conversación**. The short, accessible request should collect only the details needed for a relevant follow-up and feel like the beginning of a conversation.
- Secondary CTA: **Explore a world** / **Explorar un mundo**, guiding visitors to Integración or Sidón while Ecosat remains the central connector.
- The line a visitor should remember after 10 seconds: **Connecting worlds. Driving decisions.** / **Conectamos mundos. Impulsamos decisiones.**
- Belief ladder: Ecosat is credible; it understands operations as well as technology; it connects the physical and digital worlds; the ecosystem can adapt to an organization's context and maturity; Sidón turns operational information into visibility, control, and intelligence, with Duma as its intelligence layer; a conversation helps identify the right starting point.
- Proof on hand: more than 35 years of experience, regional reach from Chihuahua across Mexico, the United States, North America, and Central America, and experience across industrial and manufacturing, retail and QSR, food, tourism and hospitality, aerospace, government, and binational organizations. Approved experience materials include customer logos such as Emerson, Ternium, Milwaukee, Chamberlain, McDonald's, Carl's Jr., Bafar, Sunrise Confections, Grupo Xcaret, Cessna, Beechcraft, IBWC, and CILA; and technology partners including Axis Communications, Avigilon, Hanwha Vision, Panduit, exacq Technologies, Verkada, Software House, Hikvision, and HID. Real application contexts include retail loss prevention, industrial perimeter security, branch protection, and multisite corporate monitoring. Customer and partner logo authorization must be reconfirmed before launch. Do not claim unapproved case studies, testimonials, certifications, outcome metrics, or counts.

## Brand Personality

Clear, connected, and trustworthy. The experience should make complex physical and digital environments easy to understand and Ecosat credible as a long-term operational partner. Intelligence is a Sidón product outcome, personified through Duma; it is not Ecosat's overarching personality.

## Anti-references

Do not create a conventional corporate or marketing site, static brochure, sales deck, product launcher, SaaS dashboard, admin portal, card-grid catalogue, futuristic AI showcase, cyberpunk control room, gaming or metaverse interface, literal space or nautical theme, dense technology collage, spectacle-first agency portfolio, or traditional industrial site. Avoid hard shadows, gloss, saturated gradients, stock-style illustration, multicolored iconography, excessive copy, decorative motion, aggressive parallax, and motion that delays access. The intended direction is light, spacious, precise, controlled, and progressively explorable, with an abstract orbital language that connects scenes without depicting astronomy.

## Design Principles

1. **Connect before you explain.** Frame Ecosat as the central relationship between physical operations and digital intelligence, then allow each world to retain its own role.
2. **Build trust before commercial intent.** Establish operational credibility and relevance before inviting the visitor to request a conversation.
3. **Let discovery remain self-directed.** Keep exploration meaningful for users who are not ready for a commercial action, with contextual depth once they enter Integración or Sidón.
4. **Keep worlds distinct and legible.** Integración is the physical world; Sidón is the digital world; Duma exists only as Sidón's intelligence layer.
5. **Make motion serve understanding.** Scroll, transitions, and orbital relationships may guide progression, but content and interaction must never depend on precise gestures, hover, or animation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 Level AA across desktop, tablet, and mobile. Every meaningful element must be keyboard accessible with persistent visible focus, semantic structure, clear labels, logical headings, screen-reader support, and touch targets of approximately 44 by 44 CSS pixels. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; the whole narrative must remain understandable and operable without movement, parallax, or scroll-dependent effects. Do not convey essential meaning through color alone. Ensure compliant text and interface contrast, explicit form labels and errors, accessible confirmation after submission, correct Spanish and English language metadata, and appropriate alternative text. Any future video, narration, or sound requires captions or transcripts and must not autoplay.
